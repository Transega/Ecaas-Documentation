Background Information

Hi there Welcome to the technical documentation of Enabling Crop Analytics at Scale (ECAAS).

The project is an initiative by Tetra Tech, funded by Melinda and Bill Foundation whose main aim is to foster collection of Ground truth georeferenced Agriculture labelled datasets that would improve predictive analytics in smallholder agriculture through use of AI/ML and satellite imagery.

Project Implementation

The project was Implementated in two countries namely;

  1. Kenya

  2. Zambia

Crops of Interest

Various Crops were targeted this includes;

  1. Maize

  2. Potatoes

  3. Millet

  4. Sorghum

  5. Beans
